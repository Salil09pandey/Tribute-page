Sardar Vallabh Bhai Patel

The man responsible for Unity of India.

Here's a timeline of Sardar Patel's life:

  • 1875 - Sardar Patel born in Nadiad,Kheda district, Gujrat India.His father is Jhaverbhai Patel and mother is Ladba Patel.
  • 1891 - When he was only 16, Patel married to Jhaverba Patel.
  • 1897 - At the age of 22, he completed his matricution. So everyone told him as average student.
  • 1909 - His wife Jhaverba Patel expired.
  • 1911 - At the age of 36, Vallabhbhai Patel journeyed to England and enrolled at the Middle Temple Inn in London.
    Patel finished at the top of his class despite having no previous college background. He completed the 36-month course in 30 months.
  • 1917 - Patel first time meets to Mahatma Gandhi and his life totally changed. He joined Congress and became secretary of Gujrat Sabha.
  • 1920 - Patel joined Gandhi's Non-Cooperation Movement (1920) and travelled around West India to recruit 3,00,000 members. He also collected more than Rs 1.5 million for the party fund.
  • 1923 - When Mahatma Gandhi was imprisoned, it was Patel who led the Satyagraha movement in Nagpur against British law.
  • 1928 - People gives the title 'Sardar' to Vallabhbhai Patel in Bardoli Satyagrah in Gujrat.
  • 1930 - In 1930, the British arrested Sardar Patel during the Salt Satyagraha and put him on trial without witnesses.
  • 1939 - On the outbreak of World War-II, Patel supported Nehru's decision to withdraw Congress from central and provincial legislatures.
  • 1942 - Patel was at his persuasive best when he spoke at Gwalia Tank ground(now called August Kranti Maidan) in Mumbai to launch the nationwide civil disobedience movement movement in 1942 at the behest of Mahatma Gandhi. During quit India Movement(1942), the British arrested Patel. He was imprisoned with the entire Congress Working Commitee from 1942 to 1945 at the fort in Ahmednagar.
  • 1947 - In 1947 Jawahar Lal Nehru became first Prime Minister of India and Sardar Patel became first Deputy Prime Minister of India.He also became first Home Minister of India.
  • 1950 - In 1950, on December 15 Sardar Patel died.He is also known as "Iron Man Of India".
